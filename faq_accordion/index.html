FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me ? Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.
Is Frontend Mentor free ? Yes, Frontend Mentor offers a free plan that gives you access to a wide range of frontend challenges. With the free version, you can practice HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript using realistic design files. Premium challenges are also available for more advanced projects, but the free plan is more than enough to build strong frontend fundamentals and improve your coding skills.
Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio ? Yes, you can absolutely use Frontend Mentor projects in your portfolio. These challenges are designed to help developers showcase their frontend skills through real-world layouts. To make your portfolio stand out, it’s recommended to personalize your projects, improve accessibility, optimize performance, and clearly explain your development choices. This shows recruiters your problem-solving abilities and attention to detail.
How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge ? If you’re stuck on a Frontend Mentor challenge, you can get help in several ways. You can explore the official Frontend Mentor community, where developers share solutions, tips, and feedback. Reviewing other users’ approaches can help you understand different problem-solving techniques. You can also break the challenge into smaller parts and focus on one issue at a time to improve your HTML, CSS, or JavaScript skills.
